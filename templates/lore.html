Lore & Info

Welcome to the Fellowships Game, a study in Python and a tribute to timeless legends!

This game is inspired by the epic fantasy novel series The Lord of the Rings by J.R.R. Tolkien, first published in the mid‑1950s (1954–1955). In an era when paper, pen, and imagination breathed life into stories, Tolkien created a universe of myth and magic.

Now, in our modern age, our game revives that timeless spirit. Just as Tolkien's work transcends time, our game bridges classic storytelling with today's digital innovation.

Pick your hero, trust your luck (a coin flip will decide who strikes first!), and let the echoes of history guide your quest against the forces of darkness.
